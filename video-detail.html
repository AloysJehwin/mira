Ukkadam

Ukkadam Lake, located in Coimbatore, India, originated as an artificial reservoir. Constructed in the 19th century, it serves as a crucial water source for both irrigation and the city's domestic supply. The lake plays a vital role in maintaining water balance and supporting agricultural activities in the region.

About MIRA

Mira is a water supply network management system designed to efficiently monitor and control water distribution. Utilizing advanced technology, Mira ensures optimal water flow, pressure, and quality. It enables real-time data analysis, leak detection, and remote operation, enhancing the sustainability and reliability of water supply networks.
